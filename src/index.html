H.E.S.S. Galactic Plane Survey (HGPS)

Introduction

The H.E.S.S. Galactic Plane Survey (HGPS) is the first deep and wide survey of the Milky Way in TeV gamma-rays.
It was performed by the H.E.S.S. telescopes from 2004 to 2013.
The HGPS paper was accepted for publication in the A&A journal in January 2018.

On this page you can download the HGPS survey maps and source catalog data in FITS format.
A full description is given in the HGPS paper, which we also link to below.

An interactive version of the HGPS map and catalog will be made available at gamma-sky.net.
A Python package to work with the HGPS catalog is available in Gammapy (see examples).
