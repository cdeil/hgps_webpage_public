H.E.S.S. Galactic Plane Survey
HGPS paper (Jan 2018) online material

What is this?

The H.E.S.S. Galactic Plane Survey (HGPS) is the first deep and wide survey of the Milky Way in TeV gamma-rays. This webpage contains the online material for a paper describing HGPS observations taken from 2004 to 2013, that was accepted for publication in the A&A journal in January 2018.
